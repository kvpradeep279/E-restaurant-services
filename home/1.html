Our Signature dishes

Ragi mudda natikodi pulusu

Popular in the rural regions of South India,this chicken curry is rustic, spicy and vibrant. Paired with ragi sangati (finger millet flour and rice balls) it makes for a simple and satisfying meal. The only sides needed are some sliced raw onions and a generous topping of ghee.

Hyderabadi Biriyani

Hyderabadi Biryani is a flavorful, aromatic rice dish made with basmati rice, tender meat (often chicken or mutton), and a rich blend of spices. Originating from Hyderabad, India, this biryani is known for its slow-cooking "dum" method, which layers marinated meat with rice and cooks it to perfection. Garnished with fried onions, mint, and saffron, it‚Äôs a royal delicacy with a distinctive taste and fragrant aroma.

Grillfish

Grilled fish is a delicious, healthy dish where fresh fish is seasoned with herbs and spices, then cooked on a grill to achieve a smoky, charred flavor. The grilling process locks in the fish‚Äôs natural juices, giving it a tender, flaky texture with a crispy outer layer. Often garnished with lemon and fresh herbs, grilled fish is both light and flavorful.

PrawnBBQ

Prawn BBQ is a succulent seafood dish where prawns are marinated in a blend of spices, herbs, and sometimes a tangy sauce, then grilled over open flames. The grilling enhances their natural sweetness, giving them a smoky, charred flavor with a juicy, tender texture. Often served with a squeeze of lemon, prawn BBQ is a flavorful, crowd-pleasing favorite at barbecues.
